About Me

I am a writer situated in Boston, Massachusetts.

Tumultous yet rewarding transitions in my life have paved my way to where I am now.

My adolescence-era age was shaped by multitudes of things; family and friends who showed me what I was truly seeking for. I found the journeys and plots others' lives so invigorating. So it may come to no surprise that I, myself, have made my journey to Boston, Massachusetts.
