About Me

I am a writer situated in Boston, Massachusetts.

Tumultous yet rewarding transitions in my life have paved my way to where I am now.

I have made my way from the immaculate city of Arequipa, Peru to the Washington D.C. suburbs, where I have spent most of my childhood. During my adolescence, I was influenced by various people. Especially family and friends, who showed me what it means to persevere. As a curious person, I have found my wandering mind amidst the most unlikely places through books. So it may come as no surprise that I, myself, have made the journey to Boston, Massachusetts.

I am a current student at Tufts University situated in Medford, Massachusetts. I find city life more comforting now during my early twenties. I also find myself seeking new opportunities all around me to learn and collaborate with others. Having worked in industries ranging from Data Center Operations/Management to UX/UI design to writing, I have an inkling as to what my aspiring career path should look like.


If you ever want to get in contact with me, feel free to message me. My inbox is always open.


All my best,
Cristina
